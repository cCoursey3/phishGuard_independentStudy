Calling All Turtle Lovers!       You're invited to Shell-A-Brate World Turtle Day at Piedmont Wildlife Center on Thursday, May 23rd from 5:30-7:00 PM!     This all-outdoor, relaxed event will include the unveiling of our new outdoor box turtle enclosure, box turtle activity booths, Turtle Treks, photo ops, and meet-and-greets with live box turtles! You'll even get the chance to learn how to participate in our citizen science project, Turtle Trekkers.       This event is FREE with a suggested $10 donation to help cover food costs and husbandry needs for our resident box turtles.       No registration required; simply show up at Piedmont Wildlife Center in Leigh Farm Park ready to Shell-A-Brate!
