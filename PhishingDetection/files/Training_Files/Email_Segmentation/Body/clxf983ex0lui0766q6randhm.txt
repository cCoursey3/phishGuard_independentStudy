We're excited to announce that the Employee Assistance Program (E.A.P) is now open for applications, welcoming all current employees for the end-of-2023 round. Eligible employees can now request funding of up to $5,000.    The E.A.P presents a multitude of avenues for support. Employees can apply for various forms of assistance, including financial aid, relief materials, short-term counseling, referrals, and ongoing services. Embedded within our ongoing dedication at the Employee Support Service (ESS) to bolstering employee well-being, the Employee Assistance Program is designed to ease financial burdens and extend a helping hand.
